       In general about a undergraduate student

         Normally there are two class of students joining the universities and engineering colleges after clearing the Engineering entrance exam . One class is those who are from convents and whose parents are educated and have awareness about the technical education and higher studies . Another class is those who come from rural areas studied in Zilla Parishad High schools in local medium  and whose parents are illiterate and don't have the awareness about the technical education and higher studies opportunities . I am writing this section for the students belonging to the second class .

                   Some of the good options after your B.Tech are , doing a good job in some software company , doing your postgraduation in some IIT by giving GATE , doing your Management studies in some top business schools by giving CAT , going for higher education in foreign countries mostly US , CANADA , GERMANY , AUSTRALIA for M.S by giving GRE and TOEFEL .  In all the above options your knowledge in english and communication skills plays a very important role , i promise you even if you have good technical stuff and don't have good communication skills , you will not get the respect and praise you should get and instead you will loose the battle to the guys who can speak well .

            Do some homework on improving your english knowledge by reading newspapers , listening to the people who speak well etc ... .  Even if you are not giving GRE , prepare well for vocabulary , Logical reasoning , Reading comprehensions , solving puzzles , improve you writing skills as well otherwise you will loose the battle to the GRE and CAT fellows during the campus placements .

*** One important note s , see you believe it or not these 4 years of your B.Tech are the best days of your entire life so enjoy them to the fullest , bunking the classes and going to movies , roaming with  friends , going to second show movies , playing cricket , getting ragged by the seniors and ragging your juniors etc ... . Studies is only part of life , it is not everything in life , there are many more beautiful things in life so , don't miss them ( you cannot reuse time unlike space right )
