Evaluation:

The evaluation exam for ECP403: Embedded Systems Lab is cheduled on 11 Apr 2016 (Monday), between 12:00 to 1:00 PM in the regular classroom. The evaluation exam would be mostly based on multiple choice questions (MCQs) on the concepts of 8051 and its peripherals.

Record Submission:

The record for the lab must be submitted as per the insctructions and formatting detailed in the previous post (given below), by Apr 20, 2016 (11:59 PM).
